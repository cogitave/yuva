## 1. Honest Verdict on the Operator's Thesis

The thesis, compressed: *transfer the brain's operating mechanism (thinking/learning), not the corpus; the sources are already on the web; hold only what you need; train the right operation; be a learner, not an imitator; meet the need-for-education from the internet; be generative — do what you don't know.*

**The verdict, in one line: the architecture is a DEFENSIBLE and well-precedented *engineering* direction for the buildable core, it converges with a 40-year cognitive-science tradition, and it is simultaneously a CONTRARIAN BET against the scale-and-learn-end-to-end paradigm that currently owns the frontier. The most literal reading of the thesis hits four hard, named walls. The document refuses to collapse either half — and refuses to call the parameter-efficiency literature "vindication" when Sutton's own argument says those wins are the short-term-help-that-plateaus pattern.**

> **Word choice, stated once and held throughout.** This document does **not** use "vindicated" for the retrieval/decoupling line. The honest verb is **precedented**: there is a large, replicated research literature showing the *components* work in their tested regimes. Whether that direction *wins the long game* is exactly what §1.4 (the bitter lesson) puts in doubt. "Precedented" is a claim about the past; "vindicated" would be a claim about the future the evidence does not license.

### 1.1 Where the evidence is STRONG FOR THE COMPONENTS `[GROUNDED]`

**(a) Decoupling knowledge from weights is the strongest, most-replicated result in the field — for knowledge-bound tasks, and with a temporal asterisk.** The operator's "embedding everything into the model makes no sense" is not a hunch — it is the validated design principle behind an entire research line. RETRO (7.5B) matches GPT-3 / Jurassic-1 on the Pile with **25× fewer parameters** by retrieving from a 2-trillion-token non-parametric store, and its loss keeps improving as the store grows to 2T tokens — knowledge can live *outside* the weights (Borgeaud et al. 2022, arXiv:2112.04426). REALM beats the largest T5-11B Open-QA system "while being 30× smaller" (Guu et al. 2020, arXiv:2002.08909). Atlas-11B beats 540B PaLM by ~3 points on NaturalQuestions with **50× fewer parameters and 50× less pretraining compute**, and its index is updatable *without retraining* (Izacard et al. 2022, arXiv:2208.03299). A 626M RAG model set open-domain QA SOTA over a parametric-only field (Lewis et al. 2020, arXiv:2005.11401). kNN-LM states the mechanism cleanly: *"learning the similarity function between contexts is easier than predicting the next word"* (Khandelwal et al. 2020, arXiv:1911.00172). **Verdict: STRONGLY PRECEDENTED for knowledge/fact-bound tasks — but see §1.4: by Sutton's own *temporal* thesis these parameter-efficiency results are exactly the "short-term help that plateaus" pattern, and the frontier has in fact moved toward giant-model + long-context + end-to-end-learned tool use. These are evidence the components work, NOT evidence the direction wins.** `[OVERCLAIM-RISK]` on reading them as architectural vindication.

**(b) Research-not-memorize is real, and so is "train the right operation."** WebGPT (browse-and-cite) produces answers humans prefer 56% over human demonstrators and 69% over top Reddit answers — by *fetching and citing*, not by memorizing (Nakano et al. 2021, arXiv:2112.09332). Note carefully: these preference gains came from **browse-and-cite behavior executed by a large model**, not from a provenance datastructure; §2.1 must not borrow them. ReAct grounds reasoning in a live Wikipedia API, cutting hallucination and beating RL agents by +34% / +10% absolute (Yao et al. 2022, arXiv:2210.03629). Toolformer (6.7B) beats GPT-3 (175B) by calling APIs (Schick et al. 2023, arXiv:2302.04761). Search-R1 *trains* a 3B/7B model to interleave reasoning with live multi-turn search, gaining +41% / +20% over RAG — "teach it to research" as a learnable capability (Jin et al. 2025, arXiv:2503.09516) — but note its winning move is an **RL training phase that bakes the policy into weights**, which §3.3 shows Yuva's substrate structurally excludes. InstructGPT's 1.3B model is *preferred over 175B GPT-3* — 100× fewer params, the right process (Ouyang et al. 2022, arXiv:2203.02155); phi-1 (1.3B) reaches 50.6% HumanEval from "textbook-quality" data, beating models 5× its size (Gunasekar et al. 2023, arXiv:2306.11644). **Verdict: STRONGLY PRECEDENTED.**

**(c) "Learner not imitator, like a child" is Turing's own program AND a recognized CONTRARIAN bet — not naïveté, but not a vindicated path either.** `[CONTRARIAN-BET]` The thesis is, almost verbatim, Turing's 1950 child-machine: *"Why not rather try to produce one which simulates the child's? … the child brain is something like a notebook … little mechanism, and lots of blank sheets,"* divided into "the child-programme and the education process," taught by "punishments and rewards" with "communication in both directions between teacher and pupil" (Turing 1950, *Mind* 59:433–460, §7). It has a live modern research track — BabyLM trains on developmentally-plausible 10M/100M-word corpora (Warstadt, Mueller et al., arXiv:2301.11796); TinyStories shows coherent grammatical English bootstraps at <10M params from the right small corpus (Eldan & Li 2023, arXiv:2305.07759). **But this is NOT vindication and it does NOT belong in a "supported" column.** These are deliberate *toy-scale* regimes (<10M-word / <10M-param) that have **not displaced scaled pretraining** and are precisely the appealing-but-plateauing case Sutton names (§1.4). The honest classification: the developmental/child-machine framing is **a legitimate, named, ACTIVELY-CONTRARIAN research bet against scaling**, filed alongside §4.7 and §1.4 — not a result the architecture can lean on. **Verdict: the FRAMING is legitimate and named; its FEASIBILITY at language scale is an open, contrarian bet.**

**(d) Curiosity is a genuinely buildable signal — as an exploration drive only.** An agent crossed Super Mario Level-1 on *intrinsic curiosity alone* (Pathak et al. 2017, ICM, arXiv:1705.05363). Schmidhuber's compression-progress (2010, IEEE TAMD 2(3)) and Oudeyer's learning-progress IAC, which *self-organizes a developmental curriculum* (Oudeyer, Kaplan, Hafner 2007, IEEE TEC 11(2)), formalize "curiosity = where I can still improve my model." **Verdict: PRECEDENTED as a buildable *exploration* drive — with the hard caveat in §1.2(d) that it is NOT a correctness oracle, and a second caveat in §3.2 that it must never close a control loop over a confounded telemetry stream.**

**(e) Cogi's exact *shape* has a name and a 40-year lineage — which is a double-edged asset, not a pure strength.** The whole structure — small reasoner + external retrievable memory + a research action + a growing skill library + *learning by writing to memory rather than baking the web into weights* — is the explicit founding claim of CoALA: *"Learning occurs by writing information to long-term memory,"* including new code skills to procedural memory, "as an alternative/complement to updating LLM weights" (Sumers, Yao, Narasimhan, Griffiths 2023, arXiv:2309.02427). This descends from SOAR/ACT-R's procedural/semantic/episodic split with utility-gated production compilation (Laird 2022, arXiv:2205.03854). **The operator independently re-derived a four-decade cognitive-science decomposition** `[ARCHITECTURE-MATCH]`. **Honest double edge:** symbolic, hand-structured cognitive architectures (SOAR/ACT-R, and CoALA's decomposition itself) are *exactly* the human-knowledge-engineering tradition the scaling paradigm has historically out-competed (§1.4). Convergence with a respected 40-year program is real intellectual pedigree; it is **not** evidence the program beats end-to-end learning, and reading it as such inverts Sutton's actual lesson.

### 1.2 Where the thesis hits HARD WALLS `[FRONTIER]`

These are non-negotiable. Pretending any is solved would violate house discipline.

**(a) Reasoning–knowledge entanglement: retrieval offloads MEMORY, not REASONING — and the reasoning competence had to be PRETRAINED IN AT SCALE.** The cleanest controlled study: RETRO "largely outperforms GPT on knowledge-intensive tasks, but is **on par** with GPT on other [reasoning] tasks" (Wang et al. 2023, arXiv:2304.06762) — i.e. retrieval does **not help** reasoning; it does not establish that reasoning is small-corpus-trainable. Even when *all* required reasoning hops are successfully retrieved, models still fail ~58.6% of the time (NovelHopQA / survey arXiv:2506.00054). **The deeper point the thesis must absorb:** in current LLMs, the procedure/language competence that does the reasoning is *itself acquired via large-scale next-token pretraining on knowledge-rich corpora* — Ruis, Grosse et al. 2024 show reasoning draws on **procedural knowledge distributed across pretraining documents** (arXiv:2411.12580). **You cannot pretrain a procedure-rich core on a knowledge-minimal corpus**: the "knowledge-minimal-but-procedure-rich core" is an **inference-time** property of a model whose procedure was *built by* knowledge-rich pretraining at scale. **Consequence for the thesis:** "hold only as much as it NEEDS" is correct for *facts at inference time* and dangerous if read as "train the core minimally" or "the procedure is separable from the knowledge that produced it." You cannot retrieve your way out of needing a genuinely competent thinker, and you cannot *create* that thinker without scale. `[OVERCLAIM-RISK]` if M32 is under-trained on the false hope retrieval compensates.

**(b) The bootstrap floor is real and quantified — and it binds M32 directly.** Below ~7B, retrieved context frequently *hurts* — a "scaling cliff" / "paradoxical degradation" (arXiv:2603.11513) — and sub-3B models score ~**zero** on hard multi-hop web-research benchmarks like BrowseComp (arXiv:2504.12516; WebSailor arXiv:2507.02592). The small-model-punches-up results carry a hidden dependency: a 3B beating a 405B on MATH-500 *requires a 7B–72B external Process Reward Model* to verify each step — *"smaller models are more dependent on robust verifiers"* (arXiv:2502.06703). And RLVR only *elicits* reasoning already latent in the base model; it does not *teach* new reasoning from nothing (Yue et al. 2025, arXiv:2504.13837). **Direct consequence for the build, stated explicitly so the loop closes:** M32's dropped-in GGUF must be **at or above the ~7B-equivalent floor** to be a competent researcher. **"Small" in this document means small-relative-to-405B, NOT small-in-absolute-terms.** A sub-7B "small" GGUF would sit *below* the floor this section establishes and would reproduce the scaling-cliff / BrowseComp-zero failure. The floor is not "minimal knowledge" but "minimal-yet-sufficient procedure + language + a strong external verifier." You cannot push the parametric floor toward zero and still get a competent researcher.

**(c) Continual learning without catastrophic forgetting is UNSOLVED — and worsens with scale.** Naïve continual fine-tuning causes forgetting that *intensifies* from 1B→7B; a concrete sequence dropped task-1 accuracy 89.3%→67.2% (24.8% absolute) after three later tasks (Luo et al. 2023, arXiv:2308.08747; corroborated arXiv:2504.01241). This is *why every shipped LLM is frozen*. Mitigations are partial: EWC protects important weights but trades new-task plasticity (Kirkpatrick et al. 2017, PNAS, arXiv:1612.00796); replay reduces forgetting but needs stored/regenerated past data (Lesort et al. 2020, arXiv:2007.00487; Rolnick et al. 2019). **Consequence:** "learn with us, continuously" is buildable *in the memory/skill layer*, NOT in the weights of a language-competent core. The field has shifted the *locus of learning from weights to retrieval/orchestration* (Wang CSUR 2025 survey) — which is what keeping the kancell dormant does. **But see §1.4: choosing the memory locus is a real escape from forgetting AND a structural inability to do the weight-locus learning that the scaling paradigm uses. Both are true.**

**(d) "Do the unknown / be generative / BE the internet" is the OPEN FRONTIER. Never pretend otherwise.** Seq2seq models hit ~100% i.i.d. and ~0% on systematic recombination (SCAN, Lake & Baroni 2017, arXiv:1711.00350). Generation *interpolates to the mean*: AI-assisted writing is 10.7% more homogeneous collectively (arXiv:2402.01536). Verified genuinely-new knowledge currently arrives ONLY via generate-and-verify over an *external oracle* — FunSearch/AlphaEvolve improved 67 open math problems, but only because an evaluator could check each candidate (arXiv:2511.02864). Open-endedness (POET, OMNI) works in toy 2D worlds, not open-world knowledge (arXiv:1901.01753; arXiv:2306.01711). Curiosity drives *exploration*, not *correctness*, and breaks on the "noisy-TV" trap (RND arXiv:1810.12894). **Consequence:** "generative via retrieval + reasoning + skill-composition" is buildable (Voyager composes genuinely new tools, no fine-tuning — arXiv:2305.16291). "Generative via lived discovery of the nowhere-retrievable" is AGI-adjacent and unsolved. This is the load-bearing ambiguity in the thesis, and it must stay flagged.

### 1.3 The synthesis

> The operator is **right about the engineering direction for the buildable core, and right that it converges with a serious cognitive-science tradition — but the most literal reading of the thesis outruns the evidence on timeline AND runs against the grain of the dominant scaling paradigm.** Build the child-machine's *body* now — non-parametric memory + a curiosity-scored adaptive policy + a human-teacher gate + a swappable small reasoning core. But for the foreseeable term, the reasoning *core* is a pretrained model that Cogi **learns AROUND** (in memory and skills), not one Cogi raises from blank sheets. The two thesis claims that outrun the evidence — "learn language from a blank notebook" and "generate the genuinely unknown without an oracle" — are kept explicitly separate from the milestone and labeled `[FRONTIER]`. The literature says two pieces are *load-bearing*: an external correctness signal, and a knowledge-minimal-yet-procedure-rich core. **What Yuva actually secured is narrower and must be stated as such:** Yuva *structurally accommodates* both — it built the **exogenous-oracle gate** (M24) and a **swappable, small-relative-to-frontier slot** (M32). It did **not** "get right" a competent procedure-rich core: whether the GGUF dropped into the M32 slot clears the ~7B competence floor (§1.2b) is a property of the **chosen model**, not of Yuva. Yuva built the *slot and the gate*, not the *competence of the organ in the slot*.

### 1.4 The bitter lesson, confronted head-on — for the WHOLE architecture, not just curiosity `[CONTRARIAN-BET]`

This is the section the thesis most needs and the one it is most tempted to hand-wave. It does not get hand-waved here.

**The claim, stated against us at full strength.** Sutton's *Bitter Lesson* (2019) is not a remark about curiosity; it is a claim about the *entire class* of approaches this architecture belongs to. Every hand-designed structure here — the **hand-designed memory tiers** (T3/T4, HOT/forget bands), the **hand-designed retrieval scoring leaf** (recency·decay + importance + relevance), the **hand-designed curiosity signals**, the **hand-designed skill-utility promotion** (utility starts at 0, ACT-R compilation), and **CoALA's entire symbolic decomposition** — is precisely the kind of human-knowledge-encoding scaffold Sutton says "helps in the short term and is personally satisfying" but is, historically and repeatedly, **bulldozed by general methods that scale with compute and learn end-to-end.** The parameter-efficiency wins in §1.1(a) (RETRO/REALM/Atlas) are, on Sutton's *own temporal reading*, the textbook instance: a clever structured method that beats the big model *today* and is overtaken as compute and end-to-end learning scale. And the frontier has in fact moved that way — toward larger models, longer context windows that absorb the "retrieval" job into the forward pass, and tool-use **learned end-to-end** (Search-R1's RL phase) rather than orchestrated by hand. The honest reading is that this architecture is **on the historically out-scaled side of the bitter lesson.**

**Why this architecture takes the bet anyway — and what would prove it wrong.** The defense is *not* "Sutton is wrong." The defense is that Yuva's project has objectives Sutton's lesson does not price:

1. **Sovereignty and verifiability are first-class, not nice-to-haves.** The bitter lesson optimizes *capability per compute*. Yuva optimizes *capability per compute subject to a hard constraint*: every decision path must be Kani/CBMC-checkable, no-float, no-unsafe, with a tamper-evident provenance chain and a proof that the safety envelope never widens. A 405B end-to-end black box cannot satisfy that constraint *at all*. Within the feasible set defined by that constraint, structured methods are not the losing side — they are the *only* side. The architecture is a bet that **the verifiable-sovereign feasible set is worth occupying even at a capability discount.**
2. **The memory locus genuinely escapes the one place scaling is *worst*** — catastrophic forgetting *intensifies* with scale (§1.2c, arXiv:2308.08747). On the continual-learning axis specifically, "shift learning to retrieval/orchestration" is not a doomed scaffold; it is where the field itself moved (Wang CSUR 2025).
3. **The constraints are a substrate-level commitment, openly named as a bet (§3.3).** The no-gradient / no-float / weight-frozen substrate *structurally excludes* the scale-and-learn-end-to-end path. That is the bitter lesson biting at the foundation, and this document says so in plain words rather than dressing it as virtue.

**The falsifier, pre-registered.** This bet **loses** if: (a) frozen-core + external-memory + hand-tuned orchestration is decisively beaten, on the tasks Yuva targets, by an end-to-end-learned tool-using model that *also* meets the sovereignty/verifiability constraint (i.e., the constraint stops being a moat); or (b) long-context + in-weights tool-use erases the retrieval advantage entirely *and* a verifiable variant becomes feasible. If either happens, the right move is to follow the evidence, not the architecture. **The architecture is a research bet against the dominant paradigm, taken with eyes open for the sake of sovereignty and verifiability — not a claim that the structured side wins on raw capability.** Nothing downstream in this document is allowed to launder that bet into a settled best practice.

## 2. The Concrete Cogi Architecture This Implies

The thesis maps onto three organs Yuva **already built**, plus a fourth seam. This is a re-labeling of standing milestones as a recognized cognitive architecture (CoALA, arXiv:2309.02427) — with the explicit caveat (§1.4) that the recognized architecture is the historically out-scaled tradition, so the re-labeling is *orientation*, not validation.

| Cognitive role | Literature analog | **Already built in Yuva** | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Non-parametric knowledge store** (the "sources already on the web") | RETRO/RAG/REALM datastore; MemGPT tiers; Generative-Agents memory stream | **MEMORY: M13 substrate + M20 durable VirtioBlkStore + M22 provenance hash-chain** | Built, boot-verified |
| **Continual-learning organ** (learn-with-us) | CoALA "write-to-procedural-memory"; ACT-R utility gating; Reflexion; replay+EWC | **LEARNING: kancell M21 (verified fixed-point, DORMANT) + M23 experience codec (features→decision→outcome) + M24 Seldonian bake-off** | Built, **honestly gated OFF** |
| **Swappable reasoning organ** (the operating mechanism) | RETRO small core; Self-RAG 7B; Search-R1 3B/7B; NVIDIA SLM-agent thesis | **REASONING: M30 transport + M31 live Anthropic bridge + M32 local llama.cpp GGUF** | M31 first "hello" landed; M32 in-flight |
| **Growing skill library** (compose, don't bake) | CoALA procedural memory; Voyager skill library; SOAR chunking | **Capability microkernel + M18 skill-gating** | Substrate present |

**The genuine differentiators, scoped honestly and up front.** The novelty Yuva adds over standard RAG/agents is **narrow infrastructure**, not a new learning capability: (1) the **M22 provenance hash-chain** giving tamper-evidence over memory *mutations*; (2) the **Kani-verified, no-float integer scoring leaf**; (3) the **Seldonian/HCPI conjunctive activation gate** (`ACTIVATION_MARGIN=250`, `KAN_ACTIVE: bool = false` — verified in-repo at `crates/tb-encode/src/bakeoff.rs:101` and `crates/tb-hal/src/mem/mod.rs:273`). These are real but **modest** — they are *safety/provenance/verification hardening AROUND standard RAG*. They do **not** constitute an architectural advance over RAG's *learning* capability, and nothing below should be read as claiming they do.

### 2.1 MEMORY (M13/M20/M22) = the retrieval store `[ARCHITECTURE-MATCH]`

Yuva's tiered, durable memory is *structurally* the non-parametric store RETRO/RAG retrieve from. The narrow, **scoped** differentiator: **M22's provenance hash-chain makes every memory write/forget/skill-admit tamper-evidently provenanced** (an append-only 256-bit fold over mutations, kept in-RAM this milestone; the experience log is a separate chain — `crates/tb-hal/src/mem/mod.rs:904-918`). **What this is NOT:** it is *not* yet a retrieval-time citation binding wired into the answering organ, and it does **not** by itself produce WebGPT's 56%/69% human-preference gains — those came from **browse-and-cite behavior in a large model** (arXiv:2112.09332), not from a hash chain. The chain provides *tamper-evidence and citeability of writes*; turning that into WebGPT-style answer-citation is downstream orchestration work, not a landed property. Because knowledge lives in the store, Cogi can stay *fresh* by updating the VirtioBlkStore index — no re-bake of the core (the Atlas "updatable index" property, arXiv:2208.03299). This is the operational form of "meet its need-for-education from the internet." *(Bitter-lesson caveat, §1.4: long-context models increasingly absorb the retrieval job into the forward pass; the store's edge here is provenance/verifiability, not raw capability.)*

### 2.2 LEARNING (kancell M21 + M23 + M24) = the continual-learning organ, correctly DORMANT `[OPEN-IN-YUVA]` `[CONTRARIAN-BET]`

The kancell is a verified fixed-point KAN policy that ships **`KAN_ACTIVE == false`** (a compile-time const at `crates/tb-hal/src/mem/mod.rs:273`; the shadow decision is *logged only*, never on the boot decision path). M23 records the `(features → decision → outcome)` tuples — exactly the triplets experience-replay needs (Rolnick 2019, arXiv:2007.00487). M24 is a Seldonian/HCPI gate: `KAN_ACTIVE` flips `false→true` **only if** `V_lower(kancell) − V_upper(heuristic) ≥ MARGIN = 250` against an **exogenous oracle** (`crates/tb-encode/src/bakeoff.rs:38-43,101`; synthetic traces yield `gate-not-met` *by design*).

**Two true descriptions of this dormancy, held together — neither suppressed.**
- **(Honest-posture reading.)** The continual-learning literature *agrees* with M24: shift learning to the memory/orchestration layer, and a self-graded policy is "theatre" (the self-licking-ice-cream-cone confound). Keeping the cell off until a real exogenous signal clears the gate is the literature-correct posture. The exogenous-oracle requirement is supported three ways: Christiano shows the human teacher signal is *cheap* — <1% of interactions suffice (arXiv:1706.03741); InstructGPT shows preference feedback reshapes behavior more than scale (arXiv:2203.02155); FunSearch shows verified novelty needs an external evaluator (arXiv:2511.02864).
- **(Structural-inability reading — stated plainly, not buried.)** It is *equally* true that Yuva's no-gradient/no-float substrate **cannot perform weight-locus continual learning at all.** The dormancy is not only a principled refusal to self-grade; it is also the only option a substrate that excludes gradient descent *has*. Calling the limitation a virtue without naming it as a limitation would be the exact laundering this document forbids. **Both readings are true:** refusing to self-grade is genuinely the right call, *and* the substrate could not do the scaling-paradigm alternative even if it wanted to. This is the bet of §1.4, localized to the learning organ.

**Net:** keeping the kancell off behind an exogenous-oracle gate is the honest and literature-aligned posture — taken on a substrate that, by design, also leaves no other path open. `[OVERCLAIM-RISK]` neutralized: this is no longer framed as "the single most important act of honesty" simpliciter, but as a defensible posture that is *also* a named structural constraint.

### 2.3 REASONING (M30/M31/M32) — and the REFRAME of M32 `[ARCHITECTURE-MATCH]`

This is where the thesis most sharply reshapes the build. **M32's local GGUF is the small sovereign REASONING ORGAN — NOT the knowledge store.** "Small" here is bound by §1.2(b): **small-relative-to-405B, at or above the ~7B-equivalent competence floor — never small-in-absolute-terms.** Pair it with retrieval + research. The empirical *shape* is precedented: RETRO (25× fewer params at parity on knowledge tasks), Self-RAG 7B/13B beating ChatGPT on QA and fact-verification (Asai et al. 2023, arXiv:2310.11511), Search-R1 (3B/7B), and NVIDIA's explicit *heterogeneous* design — small model does the repetitive operation, big model called only for open-ended conversation (Belcak et al. 2025, arXiv:2506.02153). M31 (the Anthropic bridge) is that big-conversation escalation path; M32 is the default operation organ. The swappability *is* the thesis's "transfer the operating-mechanism, not the corpus": the reasoning organ is an interchangeable component behind a verified channel. **What Yuva secured here is the SLOT, not the OCCUPANT'S COMPETENCE** (§1.3): Yuva guarantees swappability and a small-relative-to-frontier slot; it does **not** guarantee the dropped-in model clears the floor.

**The non-negotiable reframe caveat** `[OVERCLAIM-RISK]`: keeping M32 small-relative-to-frontier is right for *knowledge*; it is **wrong if read as "train the core minimally on reasoning" or "push the core toward minimal-in-absolute-terms."** Below the ~7B-equivalent floor, retrieval *hurts* (arXiv:2603.11513) and research benchmarks collapse to ~0% (BrowseComp). And per §1.2(a), the procedure/language competence is **not separable** from the knowledge-rich pretraining that built it (arXiv:2411.12580): the corpus can leave the *weights at inference*, but the *procedure and language competence* had to be *pretrained in at scale* and cannot be conjured from a knowledge-minimal corpus. Do not cut the part retrieval does not supply.

### 2.4 SKILL LIBRARY (capability microkernel + M18) = compose, don't bake `[ARCHITECTURE-MATCH]`

Voyager's ever-growing executable-skill library is the load-bearing generalization component — *no fine-tuning*, 3.3× more items, transfers to new worlds where baselines fail (arXiv:2305.16291); ablations show ~−93% without curriculum, ~−73% without self-verification. A Yuva skill = code + NL description + embedding-slot + utility, verification-before-commit (Voyager), utility-gated promotion starting at 0 (ACT-R, arXiv:2205.03854). *(Substrate note, for honesty: the `SkillRecord.embedding` field is currently a single scalar token id in an `#[allow(dead_code)]` struct — `mem/mod.rs:864` — not a learned vector; see §3.1 on what "embedding" does and does not mean today.)* This is the operator's "its dataset could be bigger than an LLM's, but in a different way" — a library of *verified procedures*, composed at runtime, instead of a frozen parametric blob. *(Bitter-lesson caveat, §1.4: a hand-curated skill library is exactly a structured scaffold; its bet is that verifiability-per-skill is worth more than end-to-end-learned generality within the sovereign feasible set.)*

## 3. Staged Roadmap

Strictly separated: what is **BUILDABLE-NOW**, then the **gated** continual-learning activation, then **FRONTIER** items each carrying an honesty token.

> **The razor, stated once.** **BUILDABLE-NOW** = can be built today on Yuva's substrate with **zero unsolved-research dependency and zero violation of the no-float/no-network/weight-frozen discipline**, using only mechanisms that already exist in-repo or are pure-integer Kani-provable leaves. **FRONTIER-UNSOLVED** = depends on something the literature has *not* solved, OR requires a capability the substrate *structurally excludes* (gradients, floats, runtime network), OR both. Anything touching "blank-notebook language learning," "generate the nowhere-retrievable," "endogenous correctness oracle," "RL-train the policy into weights," or "live web egress" is FRONTIER. No exceptions, no blurring.

### 3.1 BUILDABLE-NOW — Milestone: the "Research Skill" (retrieval-augmented Cogi) `[GROUNDED]`

**Recommended first build. Called by its true name: this is hardened RAG + agents — standard, well-understood, and deliberately unglamorous.** A CoALA-style internal *retrieval action* over Yuva's OWN verified memory (M13/M20/M22), with the same low-risk pattern as M21/M22: a `tb-encode` pure, no-float, Kani-proven leaf + a `tb-hal` seam + a deterministic boot self-test marker (e.g. `M3x: retrieve OK`).

- **Scoring leaf — LEXICAL-ONLY, stated without euphemism `[GROUNDED]` (with one honest gap flagged).** The buildable leaf scores `recency·decay(now−tick) + importance·MemRecord.importance + relevance·IDF` where **`relevance` is exact-token BM25+ IDF over an inverted index, NOT semantic embedding similarity.** This is verified against the substrate: `recall()` generates candidates via `self.t3.lexical.get(&query)` and scores relevance as non-negative Lucene/BM25+ IDF (`crates/tb-hal/src/mem/mod.rs:1342-1374`); the kernel self-test annotates its probe token as *"a synthetic query/lexical token id (no embeddings)"* (`kernel/src/main.rs:1572`); and `SkillRecord.embedding` is a **single scalar token id** in a dead-code struct (`mem/mod.rs:864`), **not a vector.** **Therefore the earlier "Generative-Agents retrieval score" equivalence is RETRACTED for the buildable leaf:** Generative Agents (arXiv:2304.03442) uses **cosine over learned embedding vectors**, which requires a float embedding model and FPU math that **violates Yuva's no-float/no-FPU kernel discipline.** The buildable leaf delivers **lexical retrieval (exact-token BM25+), not semantic match.** Real semantic `embedding_sim` is an explicit **`[OPEN-IN-YUVA]` precursor that does not yet exist**: it needs a **no-float fixed-point semantic-similarity encoder** (a quantized integer embedding + integer dot-product leaf). Until that lands, "zero frontier exposure" applies **only to the lexical leaf**; the semantic upgrade is out of scope for this milestone and labeled accordingly. **No network, no new hardware, no floats.**
- **Self-critique loop:** the organ emits `retrieve? / relevant? / supported?` decisions (Self-RAG reflection-token analog) as host-side orchestration on the landed M31 bridge / M32 engine — faithfulness gain, no new kernel surface (arXiv:2310.11511).
- **ADAPTIVE retrieval, not always-on:** gate retrieval on uncertainty/popularity. Retrieval *hurts* ~10% of popular-entity questions and large models (PopQA arXiv:2212.10511; Helps-or-Hurts arXiv:2402.13492); adaptive retrieval recovers this AND cuts inference cost ~50%. This is itself a kancell-style "should I retrieve?" decision — but built as a **deterministic heuristic first, NOT as an activated learned policy.**
- **Reflection writes (CoALA learning, weight-free):** a periodic consolidation pass clusters M23 episodic records and writes higher-level semantic inferences back into M13 (Generative Agents reflection + Reflexion arXiv:2303.11366; sleep-time compute cuts test-time cost ~5× — arXiv:2504.13171). M22 makes every learned inference provenance-tracked at the *write* (not yet retrieval-citation) level.
- **First skill-library member:** register the research skill itself as the T4 skill object (code + desc + embedding-slot + utility), composing the existing capability microkernel + M18 gating.

**This milestone delivers the thesis's defensible core — small core + external store + research-not-memorize — with zero frontier exposure *for the lexical leaf.* It is hardened RAG + agents, not a child-machine; the grand framing of §1 does NOT describe this deliverable.**

### 3.2 GATED — Continual-Learning Activation (FAZ D / kancell) `[OPEN-IN-YUVA]`

Buildable as *mechanism*, but activation stays **gate-locked**:
- Replay buffer over M23 tuples + EWC-style importance weighting on the kancell (arXiv:2007.00487; arXiv:1612.00796) — makes "the OS learns from each iteration" *literally true for the scheduling/forget/recall policy domain ONLY* (NOT language).
- **Curiosity scoring carries the `signal=OBSERVATIONAL-NONCAUSAL` honesty token forward from M26 — and is fenced accordingly.** Computing a curiosity signal (ICM prediction-error / Schmidhuber compression-progress / Oudeyer learning-progress) over M26 exit-telemetry + the M23 stream is permitted **only to PRIORITIZE which experiences to keep/replay/observe.** It **must not** close any `exit → policy → human-query` loop autonomously. The reason is in-repo and non-negotiable: the M26 literature survey ships `signal=OBSERVATIONAL-NONCAUSAL` *precisely because* the exit stream is a confounded, non-identifiable, closed-loop signal — when the logging policy is learned from the same loop, propensities are confounded and OPE bias is non-identifiable and untestable (`docs/research/m26-exit-telemetry-literature.md:9,21`; Chaudhry et al. arXiv:2309.04222; Chaney et al. RecSys 2018). This is the *same* exogeneity problem M24 names. **A curiosity policy that auto-gated human-query timing over that confounded stream would re-import the exact self-grading confound the design exists to refuse.** Therefore: **the human-query trigger stays EXOGENOUS; curiosity may only prioritize observation/replay, never autonomously decide when to query the teacher.** The signals to compute it (age, count, `bla_raw`, envelope verdict) are already produced at each forget/recall decision and currently discarded.
- **Activation remains behind M24's `MARGIN=250` Seldonian gate against an exogenous oracle**, which expects `gate-not-met` on synthetic traces and only closes on a real M25-and-beyond human signal. **Do not flip `KAN_ACTIVE` without the cleared gate.**

### 3.3 FRONTIER — each with an honesty token, NONE claimed as solved `[FRONTIER]`

Several of these are FRONTIER *for Yuva specifically* because the substrate **structurally excludes** the winning move. That exclusion is the bitter lesson (§1.4) biting at the foundation, and it is named here as a bet's downside, not hidden:

- **Live web research** (vs. retrieval-over-own-memory): proven to work (Search-R1, WebGPT) but **blocked** by Yuva's zero-network-at-runtime CI posture — needs a gated, capability-mediated egress channel that does not yet exist (same boundary as M31 stage C / task #104 kernel-leg). The own-memory lexical research skill (§3.1) is the buildable precursor.
- **Training the core to research** (Search-R1's RL phase — *the actual winning move in that line*): requires gradient updates — **structurally outside Yuva's no-float, weight-free, LLM-agnostic discipline.** Cogi can *orchestrate* retrieve-then-reason (buildable); it **cannot** *RL-train the policy into weights* on-substrate. **This is a structural exclusion, not a deferral:** the substrate is a bet (§1.4) that the sovereign/verifiable feasible set is worth occupying *even though* it forecloses the scale-and-learn-end-to-end path the literature shows winning here.
- **Semantic (embedding) retrieval on-substrate:** requires a no-float fixed-point similarity encoder that does not yet exist (§3.1); today's leaf is lexical-only. `[OPEN-IN-YUVA]`.
- **Continual learning without catastrophic forgetting at language scale:** UNSOLVED; worsens with scale (arXiv:2308.08747). The memory/skill path *avoids* it (why the kancell stays dormant); any future weight-locus evolution inherits the open problem — which Yuva's substrate also structurally excludes.
- **Verified, envelope-preserving in-kernel online learning:** no precedent for a learning organ that updates online while *preserving proved invariants* (the safety envelope never widens). EWC/replay are empirical, float-heavy, unverified — novel research Yuva is uniquely positioned to attempt, but unsolved in the literature.
- **A trustworthy ENDOGENOUS oracle** (removing the human from the loop): does not exist; curiosity is an exploration drive, not a correctness oracle, and is hackable (noisy-TV). Until then, the human stays in the loop by design.
- **"Be generative / do the unknown / be the internet":** retrieval + recombination + skill-composition yields new *compositions* (buildable); open-ended generation of nowhere-retrievable knowledge is **AGI-adjacent and unsolved** — generation interpolates and homogenizes (arXiv:2402.01536; SCAN arXiv:1711.00350), and verified novelty needs an external oracle (arXiv:2511.02864). **This is the thesis's hardest claim and it is NOT solved. Stating otherwise would be a lie.**

## 4. What This Position Does NOT Claim

In the house honesty discipline, the negative space is as load-bearing as the claims:

1. **It does NOT claim retrieval teaches reasoning.** Retrieval offloads memorization; reasoning competence is parametric and cannot be retrieved (arXiv:2304.06762; ~58.6% multi-hop failure even with all hops retrieved).
2. **It does NOT claim the reasoning core can be arbitrarily small, nor that procedure is separable from the knowledge that built it.** There is a quantified bootstrap floor (~7B-equivalent); below it, retrieval and research *degrade* (arXiv:2603.11513; BrowseComp ~0%). "Hold only what it needs" governs *facts at inference time*, not the *procedure/language core* — and that core's competence was itself **built by knowledge-rich pretraining at scale** (Ruis/Grosse 2024, arXiv:2411.12580): you cannot pretrain a procedure-rich core on a knowledge-minimal corpus. "Small" means small-relative-to-405B, **never** small-in-absolute-terms.
3. **It does NOT claim Cogi learns language "from a blank notebook."** Every working small-core result starts from a *pretrained imitator* + retrieval/RL. The pretrained core is "genetic endowment"; "learning with us" lives in the memory/skill/kancell layer. The blank-notebook / developmental-from-scratch path is a **CONTRARIAN research bet against scaling** (§1.1c, §1.4), not a vindicated route; BabyLM/TinyStories are toy-scale curiosities that have not displaced scaled pretraining.
4. **It does NOT claim continual learning is solved.** It is unsolved and worsens with scale. The kancell stays DORMANT behind M24's exogenous-oracle gate — a posture that is *both* literature-correct *and* the only path a no-gradient substrate has (§2.2).
5. **It does NOT claim Cogi can "do what it doesn't know" in the strong sense.** Generative-via-retrieval-and-composition is buildable; generative-via-lived-discovery-of-the-nowhere-retrievable is the open frontier.
6. **It does NOT claim the small-model test-time wins are free.** They depend on a large (7B–72B) external verifier (arXiv:2502.06703) — a sovereign, cheap, trustworthy verifier is itself an open problem central to M24.
7. **It does NOT claim the developmental/curiosity scaffold — or the architecture as a whole — is guaranteed to scale or to beat the dominant paradigm.** Sutton's Bitter Lesson cautions that hand-structured approaches "always help short-term but plateau" (2019), and that caution applies to the **entire** structured architecture here — memory tiers, scoring leaf, curiosity signals, skill promotion, and the CoALA decomposition itself — not merely to curiosity. Betting the architecture on the structured side is a **research bet against scaling, taken for sovereignty/verifiability**, with the falsifier pre-registered in §1.4 — not a settled result.
8. **It does NOT claim convergence with CoALA/SOAR/ACT-R is validation.** It is *orientation and pedigree*. Symbolic cognitive architectures are the very human-knowledge-engineering tradition the scaling paradigm has historically out-competed; the convergence is a double-edged asset, not a proof the architecture wins.
9. **It does NOT claim the provenance chain produces WebGPT's preference gains, nor that the buildable retrieval is semantic.** M22 gives tamper-evidence over memory *writes* (in-RAM this milestone); WebGPT's 56%/69% came from browse-and-cite in a large model. The shippable retrieval leaf is **lexical (BM25+) only** — semantic embedding retrieval needs a no-float encoder that does not yet exist.
10. **It does NOT claim the differentiators are a new learning paradigm.** The genuine novelty (provenance chain, Kani-verified integer scoring leaf, Seldonian activation gate) is **safety/provenance/verification hardening around standard RAG** — real but modest engineering, not an architectural advance over RAG's learning capability.

**Net position:** the operator picked a **defensible engineering direction** for the buildable core and correctly diagnosed the embed-everything failure mode *for knowledge*. Yuva built recognizable organs (memory, gated learning, swappable reasoning slot, skill library) that map onto a respected 40-year cognitive-science decomposition — a decomposition that is *also* the historically out-scaled side of the bitter lesson, taken here as a deliberate sovereignty/verifiability bet (§1.4). The honest path forward is to ship the **hardened-RAG retrieval-augmented research skill** now (lexical leaf, zero frontier exposure), keep the reasoning core **small-relative-to-frontier but at-or-above the competence floor**, keep the continual-learning organ dormant behind its exogenous-oracle gate (honest posture *and* structural constraint), and hold "be generative / be the internet" as a true but **unsolved** north star — never as a claimed capability, and never as a description of what actually ships.
